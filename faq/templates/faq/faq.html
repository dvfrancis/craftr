Frequently Asked Questions

Craftr is an annual digital crafting event held online over three days each April.

It brings together crafters, artists, and makers to explore cutting-edge digital crafting techniques, including Cricut designs, laser cutting, embroidery digitisation, and 3D modelling.

Craftr 2025 runs from 21st to 23rd April 2025, with a kick-off event on the first day and a closing event on the final day.

Craftr is a fully online event, allowing crafters worldwide to participate from the comfort of their own workspace.

To register, visit the Craftr website and create an account. Once registered, you can select and enrol in classes based on your interests.
